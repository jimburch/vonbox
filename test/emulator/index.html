stateanimationcolor
idlequiet dark fill (the resting state) —
loadingsingle pixel chasing around the ring blue
pausedbreathe, 60-frame triangle amber
standbyslow breathe, 80-frame triangle warm white
playingsolid hold (~2.0s) → fade out (~1.25s) → revert to idle green
error3× flash → revert red
already_playingsoft pulse over current sustained color → revert (current)
tap_acceptedsparkle cluster → settle → revert green
bootingdim steady fill (pre-network "alive") warm white
connecting_wifislow breathe (associating) blue
connecting_mqttdot chasing the ring (reaching broker) blue
net_error: wifidouble-blink pattern (no router link) amber
net_error: brokerbreathe (router up, broker down) purple
